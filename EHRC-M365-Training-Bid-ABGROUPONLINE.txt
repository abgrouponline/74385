PART C: DELIVERY METHODOLOGY (QUALITY CRITERIA)
================================================================================

1. Training Programme Structure
--------------------------------

Phase 1: Discovery & Planning (January 2026)
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Activities:
    • Stakeholder interviews with EHRC project sponsor and key users
    • Current-state assessment: existing M365 usage, skill gaps, 
      compliance pain points
    • Review of EHRC governance policies, data handling frameworks, 
      and security requirements
    • User persona mapping to tailor training content
    • Agree success metrics and adoption KPIs

Deliverables:
    • Training Needs Analysis Report
    • Curriculum outline aligned to EHRC priorities
    • Delivery schedule and logistics plan

Phase 2: Foundation Training (February – March 2026)
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Core Microsoft 365 Applications:

    Module 1: Microsoft Teams Essentials
        • Creating teams, channels, and structured conversations
        • Secure file sharing within Teams
        • Meeting best practices and recording governance
        • Guest access controls and external collaboration

    Module 2: SharePoint & OneDrive Foundations
        • Document libraries and version control
        • Co-authoring and real-time collaboration
        • Sensitivity labels and information protection
        • Permission management and secure sharing

    Module 3: Outlook & Calendar Management
        • Email organisation and productivity
        • Shared calendars and scheduling
        • Safe email practices and phishing awareness
        • Records management and retention policies

    Module 4: Microsoft 365 Governance Essentials
        • EHRC data handling policies in practice
        • Sensitivity labels and classification
        • Secure collaboration workflows
        • Compliance checklists for regulated data

Delivery Format:
    • Live instructor-led sessions (2-hour modules)
    • Hands-on guided exercises using real-world EHRC scenarios
    • Accessible screen-share demonstrations
    • Q&A and troubleshooting support

Phase 3: Intermediate Skills (April – August 2026)
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
    Module 5: Document Lifecycle Management
        • Advanced co-authoring and track changes
        • Metadata, tagging, and search optimisation
        • Retention labels and archiving workflows
        • eDiscovery basics for compliance teams

    Module 6: Accessible Content Creation
        • Accessibility Checker in Word, Excel, PowerPoint
        • Creating WCAG 2.2 AA compliant documents
        • Alt text, reading order, and semantic structure
        • Inclusive design principles

    Module 7: Secure Collaboration Patterns
        • External sharing controls and audit trails
        • Data loss prevention (DLP) awareness
        • Working with regulated/sensitive information
        • Safe use of personal devices and mobile apps

Phase 4: Embedded Practice & Champions (September – October 2026)
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
    • Role-based coaching sessions for power users
    • Champion network setup: identify and train internal advocates
    • Scenario-based workshops tailored to EHRC use cases
    • Drop-in support clinics for ongoing questions

Phase 5: Impact Review & Handover (November – December 2026)
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
    • Adoption metrics review (usage analytics, feedback surveys)
    • Lessons learned and continuous improvement recommendations
    • Handover playbooks for EHRC champions to sustain adoption
    • Extension option planning (if triggered)
